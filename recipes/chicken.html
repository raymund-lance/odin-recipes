Soy marinated chicken

Tender and juicy chicken that is rich in flavors, easy to cook and not time consuming recipe

Ingredients

  • 2pcs chicken quarter legs
  • 1tsp ground black pepper
  • 1Tbsp brown sugar
  • 5 cloves of garlic
  • 2 stalks of lemon grass
  • 4Tbsp of soy sauce
  • 1Tbsp of oyster sauce(optional)
  • 2pcs calamansi juice

Procedure

  1. Marinate for atleast 1 hour
  2. Put small amount of oil on a heat pan
  3. Cook the chicken in low heat
  4. After halfway cooking pour the left over sauce on the pan
  5. Close the lid and simmer it until liquid is reduced
  6. Add 2Tbsp of butter
  7. Fry the chicken until cooked
